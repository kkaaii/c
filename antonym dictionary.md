# Atonym Dictionary
Atonyms with same length of characters.

## 3 characters
* cmd - dat
* get - set
* get - put
* rcv - snd
* src - dst

## 4 characters
* head - tail
* next - prev

## 5 characters
* lower - upper

## 6 charaters
* footer - header
* opcode - status

## 7 characters
* dequeue - enqueue

## 8 characters
* incoming - outgoing
